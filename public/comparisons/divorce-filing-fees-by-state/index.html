Divorce Filing Fees by State

Information Verified: January 2026 Sources: Official State Court Websites Content Type: Informational Resource (Not Legal Advice)

Divorce filing fees vary significantly across the United States. Each state sets its own court filing fees, and costs can also differ between counties within the same state. Filing fees typically range from under $200 to over $500 depending on the jurisdiction. Understanding the expected cost before filing helps you plan and determine whether you may qualify for a fee waiver.

Filing Fee Waivers and Cost Reduction Options

Many individuals filing for divorce may qualify for a court filing fee waiver based on income and financial circumstances. State courts typically provide official fee waiver request forms that allow eligible filers to avoid paying the filing fee entirely.

Each InFormer Divorce Form Kit includes the appropriate court fee waiver request forms for the specific state where the divorce is filed. These forms are the same official documents used by the courts and are included at no additional cost to help individuals who qualify request a waiver of filing fees.
