Editorial & Verification Policy

InFormer Legal Forms is committed to providing accurate, current, and clearly sourced information about divorce filing requirements across all 50 U.S. states.

Information Sources

All divorce filing information published on InFormer is based on publicly available court records, official state judiciary websites, and government-published legal resources. We do not rely on third-party legal blogs, user-generated content, or unverified secondary sources for filing requirements, fee schedules, or procedural details.

Each state page includes citations linking directly to the official court or judiciary sources used. These citations are provided so that users can independently verify the information presented.

Periodic Review Process

Divorce filing requirements, court fees, and procedural rules can change when state legislatures pass new laws or courts update their administrative orders. InFormer reviews state-specific content on a periodic basis to identify and reflect these changes.

Our review process includes checking official judiciary websites for updated fee schedules, revised filing procedures, and changes to residency or waiting period requirements. When updates are identified, the affected state pages are revised accordingly.

Last Verified Dates

Each state page displays a "Last Verified" date directly below the page heading. This date indicates when the information on that page was most recently reviewed against official state sources.

A "Last Verified" date does not guarantee that all information is current at the time of reading. Court fees, procedural rules, and statutory requirements may change between verification cycles. Users are encouraged to confirm critical details directly with their local court clerk's office before filing.

InFormer Is Not a Law Firm

InFormer Legal Forms is not a law firm, legal practice, or attorney referral service. We do not employ or retain attorneys to prepare documents on behalf of users. Our products are self-help legal form kits designed for individuals who are handling their own uncontested divorce filings.

No Legal Advice Provided

Nothing on this website constitutes legal advice. The information provided is for general informational purposes only and should not be interpreted as a substitute for professional legal counsel. InFormer does not evaluate the merits of any individual case, provide opinions on legal strategy, or make recommendations about how to proceed with a divorce filing.

Individuals with contested divorces, complex asset divisions, custody disputes, or questions about their legal rights should consult a licensed attorney in their state.

Corrections and Updates

If you believe any information on this site is inaccurate or outdated, we encourage you to contact us. We take factual accuracy seriously and will review reported discrepancies against official sources promptly.
