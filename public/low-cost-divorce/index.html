Can’t Afford a Divorce Lawyer?

You may still be able to file at reduced cost — or possibly $0 depending on court approval.

Many people assume divorce requires thousands in legal fees. If your situation is uncontested, you may be able to handle it yourself using standard court forms and general guidance.

What this provides:

  • State-specific divorce form kits
  • Structured step-by-step filing guidance
  • Common forms used in your state
  • Filing order information
  • Official fee waiver request forms (approval determined by the court)

This is a good fit if:

  • You and your spouse generally agree
  • You are handling the process yourself
  • You want a lower-cost option
  • You want a clear starting point

This may NOT be the right fit if:

  • Your case is highly contested
  • There are complex legal disputes
  • You need legal representation

Important:

Court filing fees can cost hundreds of dollars. Some individuals may qualify for a fee waiver based on income.

Each kit includes official fee waiver request forms so you can apply if eligible.
