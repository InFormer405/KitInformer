Why State-Specific Divorce Forms Matter

Filing divorce paperwork using incorrect forms, outdated residency timelines, or inaccurate fee information can delay proceedings or result in rejection by the court. Each state has unique statutory requirements that must be followed for a filing to be accepted.

InFormer divorce form kits are structured to align with the specific requirements of each state, including the correct forms, filing instructions, and fee waiver documentation. Kits are available for cases with and without minor children.
